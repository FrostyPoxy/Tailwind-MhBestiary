Species Description

Monsters that drape themselves in their great wings, like a black overcoat. They have no eye sensory organs, so instead they use a scattering of fine scales from their wings to locate prey. When their sensory capacity increases, antennae appear on their heads and they unfurl their wings, entering the aggressive Frenzy State. Their black scales can Frenzy other creatures as well, making them fearsome adversaries.

Information

Gore Magala is a Large Monster in Monster Hunter Wilds (MHWilds). Gore Magala is a pitch-black monster that resides in the Iceshard Cliffs. Large Monsters such as Gore Magala are hostile and are usually the primary objective of Hunts. They provide valuable Materials when defeated, that allow Hunters to craft gear.

Aliments Description Effectiveness

Poison

Poison slowly drains the monster's health for a short amount of time

Sleep

The monster slumbers a short while. Attacking will awaken it, but the first attack will do more damage.

Paralysis

The monster is paralyzed and cannot move for a short amount of time

Blastblight

Blast element builds as you attack. Once a certain threshold is reached, it detonates, causing damage.

Stun

Attacking a monster's head many times with a blunt weapon will KO them, leaving them wide open.

Exhaust

Once blunt damage buildup reaches a certain level, the monster will lose a portion of its stamina.

Gore Magala Moveset

Aerial Dive

Aerial Dive

While Frenzied and airborne, the Gore Magala will flap its wings once, and then twist its body to fly higher. After a spin, it will crash all of its weight on a target.

Aerial Strike

While Frenzied, Gore Magala will stand up on its hind legs and will jump at the same time it flaps its wings. While in the air, it will pull back its head quickly, then unleash a cloud of black smoke from its mouth from left to right. There's a variation of this move where it will strike directly in front of the monster, and then to the left to right, or right to left.

Bite

After repositioning itself, the Gore Magala will shift its body to the left as it raises its right arm. Then, it will follow up with a quick charge to bite the targets in front of it. Hunters can get blown away when hit directly.

Frenzy Arc

The monster will let out a growl as it raises both wings above its head. Gore Magala will swing its head to the right with its mouth wide open, and then quickly release Frenzy as it swings its head to the opposite side. This attack can throw Hunters off balance and can inflict the Frenzy: Onset. This monster can also do the same move from the other side.

Frenzy Projectile

The Gore Magala will start wiggling its large wings on its sides and will quickly rise up on its hind legs as it pulls back its head. A black smoke will form on its mouth and will shoot out Frenzy Virus targeted at hunters.

Frenzy Smoke

The Gore Magala will pull back its head on its right side. After a quick pause, it will violently shake its head and unleash a Frenzy Smoke to the right, left, and then center. The last instance will push its body backward.

Headbutt

When hunters are attacking its backside and the Gore Magala is facing away, its face will turn its sides as it lets out a roar. After a quick pause, it will pivot and slam the targets with its head.

Reposition

When the Gore Magala is being attacked from all sides, it will let out a low roar and quickly pivot on all fours to push itself upward.

Ring of Frenzy

While on all fours, the Gore Magala will shriek while pulling back its body. Take note of the claws at the tip of its wings where you can see it wiggling. After a quick pause, it will unleash a Frenzy aimed at a hunter that is usually attacking the face of Gore Magala. This attack leaves a ring of black smoke that disperses after a few seconds. When enraged, it will blow a second instance of smoke at the same spot.

Rush

While on all fours, the Gore Magala will slide both arms to the right side, and then raise the right arm in the last frame. It will then jump forward and rush towards a target taking about 2-3 steps before it turns around.

Smoke Slam

The Gore Magala will shift its weight to the left and raise the wing above its head. It will hold this position while the monster is growling and once it stops, it will swing its head to the left as it releases a cloud of smoke from its mouth. This impact can blow away hunters when hit directly.

Upward Wing Strike

While on all fours, the Gore Magala will pull back its body to the right and then, it will stand up on its hind legs. After a quick pause, it will rush forward and make an Upward motion with its Right Wing. This move can disrupt a Hunter's balance and cause them to drop to the ground. It can do the same move on the other side.

Wing Backstep

While growling, the Gore Magala will take two backsteps and then raise its right arm and wing at the last frame. Once the wing reaches the peak, it will strike forward, then pull it back towards itself. The force of this movement will cause the Gore Magala to flip on its side.

Wing Strike to Backstep

After growling quickly, it will shift its weight to the left as it raises both wings. After a quick pause, it will take a step towards the left, strike forward using its right wing, and slide that same wing backward, as it takes another backstep. This attack is sometimes followed up with a Frenzy Arc.

Winged Claw Strike

When hunters are gathered at the feet of the Gore Magala, it will retaliate with this move that starts by flapping its wings once, and then the claws of the attacking wing will wiggle right before it scratches the ground. Then, from above, it will strike downward, and end the move with an Upward swing. It can repeat this move by doing it from the other side.

Wing Strike

The Gore Magala will shift its weight to the right and will slowly raise its right wing. After a brief pause, it will take a step forward as it slams the tip of its wings (with claws) toward the ground. This move can inflict Frenzy: Onset and blow away hunters when hit directly.

Species Description

Bird wyverns with impressive beaks and large ears. They tend to be skittish but will sometimes initiate attacks on a perceived threat. Yian Kut-Ku arrive in large groups during the season of Plenty in the Scarlet Forest. The absence of eggs or young suggests that the purpose of this is not to breed, but rather seek out mates and nourishment.

Information

Yian Kut-Ku is a Large Monster in Monster Hunter Wilds (MHWilds). Yian Kut-Ku is a bird wyvern with a massive beak and large ears, which are incredibly sensitive to loud noises. Large Monsters such as Yian Kut-Ku are hostile and are usually the primary objective of Hunts. They provide valuable Materials when defeated, that allow Hunters to craft gear.

Yian Kut-Ku Moveset

Aerial Maneuver

When cornered, this monster will use its wings to flap forward. The force will push them backward and give them space to move. It can follow up this move with a Fiery Ball.

Beak Attack

The Yian Kut-Ku will raise both of its arms/wings above its head and will start poking its beak on the ground to attack its target. It can repeat this move up to 4 times consecutively.

Bite

When the hunters attack its sides, the Yian Kut-Ku will attempt to bite them. It will open up its mouth, pause momentarily, and then sweep its head onto either side to throw Hunters off balance. They can follow up this attack with a Tail Sweep, which is a move where they swing their Tail toward the Hunters.

Charge to Tail Sweep

The Yian Kut-Ku will charge at Hunters as soon as they see them. They will start running with their wings high and above their head. Once they reach the hunter's position, they will immediately follow up with a Tail Sweep as they turn their body.

Enraged Beak Attack

This is the same as the Beak Attack listed above, but instead of 4, the maximum instance is raised to 6, and it will finish the move with a Jumping Beak Attack, where it will jump from its previous position, into its target's position to do another Beak Attack.

Fiery Ball

The Yian Kut-Ku will tilt its head to the left as it raises both of its wings. Then, a glowing orange will start to form around its mouth, and after a while, it will fire it at its target. This is an attack reserved for distant hunters. It can do this move up to 2 times. When enraged, the maximum instance is raised to 4.

Fiery Charge

This is a modified charge where the Yian Kut-Ku will run towards a hunter, with its head turning from side to side, while fire pours out of its mouth.

Jumping Beak Attack

The Yian Kut-Ku will jump towards a hunter's position to do a Beak Attack.

Large Fiery Ball

This is a modified Fiery Ball attack. This move starts the same, but it will pause longer before it spews the Fiery Ball. Then, upon reaching the ground, the fire will spread and linger for a couple of seconds.

Lowered Charge

This is a variation of the charge where the Yian Kut-Ku lowers its face on the ground and pushes it with its legs towards a hunter. Once it reaches its destination, it will swing its head upwards. This attack can throw units off balance when hit directly.

Tail Sweep

The Yian Kut-Ku will sweep its tail against Hunters that are attacking it. This move can throw hunters off balance when hit directly. It can do this move up to 3 times consecutively.

Species Description

A massive Guardian that has been feeding off the Dragontorch's energy. Its body is encased in crystallized Wyvern Milk, allowing it to regenerate. Zoh Shia, like other Guardians, excels at energy attacks and can produce repeated explosions to crystallize the whole area. Beneath its white Guardian coat, its body pulses with dark recollection - which you may see, should it reveal its full power from the days of yore.

Information

Zoh Shia is a Large Monster in Monster Hunter Wilds (MHWilds). Zoh Shia is a massive guardian that excels at energy attacks and can produce repeated explosions. Large Monsters such as Zoh Shia are hostile and are usually the primary objective of Hunts. They provide valuable Materials when defeated, that allow Hunters to craft gear.

Aliments Description Effectiveness

Poison

Poison slowly drains the monster's health for a short amount of time

Sleep

The monster slumbers a short while. Attacking will awaken it, but the first attack will do more damage.

Paralysis

The monster is paralyzed and cannot move for a short amount of time

Blastblight

Blast element builds as you attack. Once a certain threshold is reached, it detonates, causing damage.

Stun

Attacking a monster's head many times with a blunt weapon will KO them, leaving them wide open.

Exhaust

Once blunt damage buildup reaches a certain level, the monster will lose a portion of its stamina.

Screamer pod

Some monsters are startled by loud noises, prompting specific reactions from them.

Shock Trap

If a monster triggers a shock trap, it will be immobilized and open to attack for a short amount of time.

Pitfall Trap

Zoh Shia Moveset

Arm Blast

The Zoh Shia will plant its claws firmly on the ground as the white glow brightens around its arm. After a quick pause, it will pull back its arm and cause a blast on the ground.

  • Arm Stomp

    The Arm Stomp is the simple version of the Arm Blast without the glowing white on its arm, and it also doesn't cause any blasts or explosions.

Arm Swing

While on all fours, the Zoh Shia will quickly turn on its side, and it will use its wing to cover its face. While in this position, the wing tip will start to glow white. After a quick pause, Zoh Shia will swing its wing across on its side. This attack can cause an explosion. (There's also a variation of this attack that does not cause any blast or explosion, so there will be no white glows on its wing).

Crossed Wingblade

The Zoh Shia will start howling and the tip of its wings will start to glow white and the monster will slowly rise to stand on its hindlegs. Once stood, the Zoh Shia will slam both wings downward and cause a blast in a moderately large area directly in front of it.

Fireball

While enraged, Zoh Shia will rise to stand on its hind legs while an orange glow forms inside its mouth. Once stood, it will unleash a Fireball directly in front of it. This attack also leaves a trail of fire on the ground.

Flame Ring

When enraged, the Zoh Shia will rise to stand on its hind legs. Once stood, it will let out a roar and its head will start to glow orange-red. After a while, it will let out a high-pitched sound and then a roar. It will quickly face the ground directly beneath it, and unleash a Flame Breath forming a Flame Ring on its feet.

Infernal Blast

The Zoh Shia's claws will turn orange, and it will plant both claws into the ground while letting out a high-pitched sound. The flames will travel from its body and leak into the ground directly in front of it. After a while, Zoh Shia will release a blast of orange flames that travel further.

Inferno

While standing on its hindlegs, the monster will unleash a straight line of flame while it swings its head around, covering the zone in flames. After a while, the entire area will explode in flames.

  • There's a version of this move where the initial move (Zone Blast) only affects a smaller area. In this version, Zoh Shia will skip the part where it swings its head around, and instead, will aim the flame breath directly below it, causing the white shards to explode.

Overhead Smash

The right arm of Zoh Shia will begin to glow and the monster will raise it above its head while letting out a roar. Once the arm peaks above its head, Zoh Shia will smash it onto the ground causing a blast.

Relentless Charge

While on all fours, Zoh Shia will roar as it takes 3 steps back. After that, it will raise its right arm above its head and will charge forward ramming its head and body against its targets. Once it reaches its destination, it will pivot its body to face the opposite direction.

Roar

The Zoh Shia can let out a roar to interrupt the Hunter's actions.

Tail Swing

While facing away from its targets, the Zoh Shia will slightly raise its tail above its body right before it extends it to swing it around.

Transformed Fireball

This is a move that Zoh Shia does after its transformation. While on all fours, the Zoh Shia will appear to "shake off" its head as an orange glow forms inside its mouth. Its wings are up and high on both sides and it will let out a roar while facing the front. After this roar, it will release a fireball in front of it, to the right, and then a close-range Fireball in front of it.

  • Charged-Up Fireball

    This is a follow-up attack that Zoh Shia does after it transforms. After releasing 3 Fireballs, Zoh Shia will rise to stand up on its hind legs while the orange glow forms inside its mouth. Once it stands, the orange glow will keep getting bigger and brighter, and then it will release a Charged-Up Fireball that can reach a longer distance. It can do this move up to 2 times consecutively. This attack can blow away hunters when hit directly.

Transformed Wing Blast

Zoh Shia will clench the claws on its wing as it glows orange. After a quick pause, it will plant its claws firmly on the ground as the orange glow brightens around its wing. Soon after, the orange glow will leak into the ground, causing a blast as the monster pulls back its arms. The Blast will explode into multiple fireballs that will travel further.

Transformed Wing Strike

This is a move that Zoh Shia does after its transformation. While roaring, Zoh Shia will raise both wings (claws) above its body. The right wing will start to glow orange and soon after, it will strike the ground in front of it. Then, it will strike again using the same wing, before switching to the other side.

White Ground Fissure

The Zoh Shia's wings will turn white, and it will plant both claws into the ground while letting out a high-pitched sound. The white energy will travel from its body and leak into the ground directly in front of it. After a while, the white energy will explode, leaving a fissure. The force of this attack will cause Zoh Shia's body to be pushed backwards.

White Ground Fissure (2.0)

This is a variation of the White Ground Fissure where the Zoh Shia will scratch the ground sideways with both claws. So, instead of the fissure traveling in front of it, the White Energy will travel in a straight line across, leaving a long white fissure on the ground.

Wing Strike and Sweep

The Zoh Shia will let out a roar as it plants the tip of its wing (with claws) onto the ground. After the roar, it will pull it backwards to try and hit the hunters near its body.

Wing Strike

The Zoh Shia will step forward and then quickly strike the tip of its left wing (with claws onto the ground). As it pulls back the left wing, it will charge up the right wing, and then strike the ground while glowing white. The second attack can cause a Blast that can blow hunters away.

Zone Blast to Inferno

The Zoh Shia will rise to stand on its hindlegs while it looks above. After a while, it will quickly fly upward and stay airborne for a few seconds, releasing white energy in the zone. Once it lands, it will start to go down on all fours, and it will release more white energy. After it charges up, this move will cause a zone-wide blast, creating large shards of white energy to form. Then, Zoh Shia will quickly follow up with Inferno, where it will unleash a straight line of flame while it swings its head around. This will cause the entire zone to go up in flames.

Species Description

Ajarakan is a Large in Monster Hunter Wilds (MHWilds). Ajarakan is a fanged beast that may induce Fireblight, and it inhabits Oilwell Basin and Ruins of Wyveria. Large Monsters such as Ajarakan are hostile and are usually the primary objective of Hunts. They provide valuable Materials when defeated, that allow Hunters to craft gear.

Information

Characteristic by their highly developed carapaces, Ajarakan wrap themselves in molten metal that they rub together to heat up and create explosions. Presumably due to the friction of the metal, they can reach high enough temperatures to melt even the ground or bullets. They can also use their strong limbs to grip onto the ceiling and move around freely, so a hunter must watch out from every angle, including above.

Aliments Description Effectiveness

Poison

Poison slowly drains the monster's health for a short amount of time

Sleep

The monster slumbers a short while. Attacking will awaken it, but the first attack will do more damage.

Paralysis

The monster is paralyzed and cannot move for a short amount of time

Blastblight

Blast element builds as you attack. Once a certain threshold is reached, it detonates, causing damage.

Stun

Attacking a monster's head many times with a blunt weapon will KO them, leaving them wide open.

Exhaust

Once blunt damage buildup reaches a certain level, the monster will lose a portion of its stamina.

Nu Udra Moveset

Charge Attack

In its normal state, the Nu Udra can dash across the area to charge at targets. The movement is much slower compared to the Fiery state.

Fiery Flame

The head of the Nu Udra will start to glow blue, and after a while, the flame will surge throughout its body.

Flame Breath

Nu Udra breathes fire on the ground. The fire lasts for a few seconds and covers a medium-sized area.

Fiery Barrage

Nu Udra will gather rocks beneath it, cover them in fire, and then throw them in an area around it. The rocks will explode upon hitting the ground and leave trails of fire.

Fiery Charge

While Nu Udra is in its fiery state, it will dash across the zone to charge at units in front of it.

Fiery Spin

During the flame state, the Nu Udra will gather its tentacles around its body and use them to spin in place, while other tentacles will sweep to attack nearby targets.

Fiery Tentacle Slam

Improved and Fiery version of the Tentacle Slam. Using one of its tentacles, the Nu Udra will reach forward and slam it on the ground.

Fiery Tentacle Sweep

Improved and Fiery version of the Tentacle Sweep. The Nu Udra will sweep one of its tentacles across the area around it. When hit, it can cause the hunter to lose balance.

Fiery Tentacle Swing

Improved and Fiery version of the Tentacle Swing. After raising one of its tentacles for a few seconds, the Nu Udra will swing it across the ground.

Tentacle Slam

Using one of its tentacles, the Nu Udra will reach forward and slam it on the ground.

Tentacle Sweep

The Nu Udra will sweep one of its tentacles across the area around it. When hit, it can cause the hunter to lose balance.

Tentacle Swing

After raising one of its tentacles for a few seconds, the Nu Udra will swing it across the ground.

Throw Attack

Nu Udra will collect rock on one of its tentacles and then fling it at a target. During the fiery state, this rock will explode upon hitting the ground. It can perform this move up to 2 times in succession.

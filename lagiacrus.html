Species Description

Leviathans that exist atop the vast aquatic food chain. Lagiacrus generate electricity via their hides to store in their spinal organs, and are capable of discharging a current strong enough to boil the seawater around them. They have been sighted fighting other monsters in the Scarlet Forest, presumably drawn in from other regions in search of prey and more fertile environs.

Information

Lagiacrus (aka 海竜) is a Large Monster in Monster Hunter Wilds (MHWilds), included in the Free Title Update 2 on June 30, 2025. To unlock the extra mission, you must be at least HR 31 and have completed both the main mission "A World Turned Upside Down" and the side mission "Forest Doshaguma." Large Monsters such as Lagiacrus are hostile and are usually the primary objective of Hunts. They provide valuable Materials when defeated, that allow Hunters to craft gear.

Aliments Description Effectiveness

Poison

Poison slowly drains the monster's health for a short amount of time

Sleep

The monster slumbers a short while. Attacking will awaken it, but the first attack will do more damage.

Paralysis

The monster is paralyzed and cannot move for a short amount of time

Blastblight

Blast element builds as you attack. Once a certain threshold is reached, it detonates, causing damage.

Stun

Attacking a monster's head many times with a blunt weapon will KO them, leaving them wide open.

Exhaust

Once blunt damage buildup reaches a certain level, the monster will lose a portion of its stamina.

Lagiacrus Moveset

Ball Lightning (Underwater)

Lagiacrus unleashes multiple balls of lightning underwater, zapping hunters that are hit, applying Thunderblight

Lightning Dive (Underwater)

Lagiacrus roars, then quickly dives toward the hunter's direction with lightning around its body. Players hit by the dive have Thunderblight applied to them.

Lightning Tail Whip (Underwater)

Lagiacrus charges up while generating lightning on its back, then whips its tail toward the player's direction. Players hit with the tail have Thunderblight applied to them.

Lightning Vortex (Underwater)

Lagiacrus charges up, then spins quickly in an area, generating a whirlpool imbued with lightning that pulls hunters towards the center. After a while, the whirlpool will release a discharge of lightning in a small area, applying Thunderblight to players caught in the explosion.

Spark (On Land)

Lagiacrus's jaw momentarily glows, then throws a small projectile of lightning on the ground that spreads in a small straight line towards the side.

Charged Tantrum (On Land)

Lagiacrus's back glows, then quickly dives toward the hunter's direction. Then, Lagiacrus will use its body to tackle sideways.

Bite and Whip (On Land)

Lagiacrus will bite at the player, then whip its tail as a follow-up attack.

Lunging Bite (On Land)

Lagiacrus lunges forward as it attempts to bite the hunter.

Lightning Tendrils (On Land)

Lagiacrus's back will start to glow as it charges up an attack, then discharges multiple lightning arcs around it, dealing multiple instances of damage. Players hit by the arcs will have Thunderblight applied to them.

Rolling Tail Whip (On Land)

Lagiacrus uses its whole body to roll toward the hunter, closing the distance between them. It will then use its tail to whip at the player to finish the sequence.

Charged Tail Whip Combo (On Land)

Lagiacrus faces the player sideways, then uses its tail to whip at the player while releasing a lightning bolt. It will then turn to its other side, then do the same sequence.

Standing Bite (On Land)

Lagiacrus stands on its hind legs, then lunges forward as it bites and releases multiple Lightning strikes around it.

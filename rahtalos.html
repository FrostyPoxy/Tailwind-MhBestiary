Species Description

Fearsome male wyverns known as the "King of the Skies." Alongside the Rathian, they stake out a wide territory around their nests, and it is the male's role to watch from above. Armed with powerful poisonous claws and fiery breath, Rathalos use their aerial prowess to descend to invaders.

Information

Rathalos is a Large Monster in Monster Hunter Wilds (MHWilds). Rathalos is a Flying Wyvern that inflicts fire and poison status effects. It is the flagship monster of the series. Large Monsters such as Rathalos are hostile and are usually the primary objective of Hunts. They provide valuable Materials when defeated, that allow Hunters to craft gear.

Aliments Description Effectiveness

Poison

Poison slowly drains the monster's health for a short amount of time

Sleep

The monster slumbers a short while. Attacking will awaken it, but the first attack will do more damage.

Paralysis

The monster is paralyzed and cannot move for a short amount of time

Blastblight

Blast element builds as you attack. Once a certain threshold is reached, it detonates, causing damage.

Stun

Attacking a monster's head many times with a blunt weapon will KO them, leaving them wide open.

Exhaust

Once blunt damage buildup reaches a certain level, the monster will lose a portion of its stamina.

Rathalos Moveset

Aerial Flame Sweep

While airborne, Rathalos will pull back both wings and raise its head while turning it to the right. After a quick pause, it will unleash its fiery breath that starts from the right going to the left. Rathalos can also do this move starting from the opposite side.

Aerial Flame Field

While airborne, Rathalos will let out a roar as an orange glow forms inside its mouth. It will pull back its head and both wings on its sides. After a quick pause, it will aim at the ground directly beneath it to unleash a fiery breath. It will pause before swinging its head upwards. This attack leaves the ground burning for a few seconds.

Aerial Fireball

Rathalos takes flight, and then swings its body around with flames growing from its mouth. After a short while, it will launch 2 Fireballs at a target.

Aerial Strafe

While airborne, Rathalos will frequently reposition itself by flapping its wings.

Aerial Tail Thrust

While in the air, Rathalos will swing its tail backward, and then quickly thrust it forward.

Fireball to Swoop

Rathalos raises its head and wings as an orange glow forms inside its mouth. It will then shoot a fireball from its mouth, directly at the area in front of it while flapping its wings to push itself upward. Once airborne, Rathalos will swoop to attack a target in front of it.

Fireball

Rathalos raises its head and wings as the flames in its mouth glow brighter. After a while, he releases a fireball that travels in a straight line toward a target.

Flame Breath

Rathalos takes a step back with both wings extended on the sides. An orange glow will start to form inside its mouth and it will pull back its head as it takes another step backward. Rathalos will release Flame Breath in front of it and then to the left. When enraged, it can release up to 3 Flame Breaths consecutively.

Ruthless Dash

Rathalos dashes in a straight line towards its targets to trample them. Rathalos will take about 3-4 steps before stopping.

Swoop Attack

While airborne, Rathalos will swoop down at a target with its talons leading the landing.

Tail Sweep

Rathalos turns its head to its side while it prepares for the move. After a brief pause, Rathalos sweeps its tail behind him as his body turns. It can follow up with a second sweep, returning it to its original position. The sweep has a deceptively large radius and may affect a larger area than it appears.

Talon Assault

While airborne, Rathalos will flap its wings to push itself backward. Then, it will flap its wings once again to push itself forward to assault with both talons, before it lands on the ground.

Talon Strike

Rathalos quickly takes flight by jumping as it flaps its wings to bring itself airborne. Then, it will swoop down and strike a target using its talons.

Species Description

Ajarakan is a Large Monster in Monster Hunter Wilds (MHWilds). Ajarakan is a fanged beast that may induce Fireblight, and it inhabits Oilwell Basin and Ruins of Wyveria. Large Monsters such as Ajarakan are hostile and are usually the primary objective of Hunts. They provide valuable Materials when defeated, that allow Hunters to craft gear.

Information

Nerscylla is a Large Monster in Monster Hunter Wilds (MHWilds). Nerscylla is a Temnoceran can be found in Oilwell Basin, Iceshard Cliffs and Ruins of Wyveria, and can inflict multiple status effects. Large Monsters such as Nerscylla are hostile and are usually the primary objective of Hunts. They provide valuable Materials when defeated, that allow Hunters to craft gear.

Aliments Description Effectiveness

Poison

Poison slowly drains the monster's health for a short amount of time

Sleep

The monster slumbers a short while. Attacking will awaken it, but the first attack will do more damage.

Paralysis

The monster is paralyzed and cannot move for a short amount of time

Blastblight

Blast element builds as you attack. Once a certain threshold is reached, it detonates, causing damage.

Stun

Attacking a monster's head many times with a blunt weapon will KO them, leaving them wide open.

Exhaust

Once blunt damage buildup reaches a certain level, the monster will lose a portion of its stamina.

Items Description Effectiveness

Flash Pod

Strong Flashes will dizzy or disorient a monster for a short period of time.

Screamer pod

Some monsters are startled by loud noises, prompting specific reactions from them.

Shock Trap

If a monster triggers a shock trap, it will be immobilized and open to attack for a short amount of time.

Pitfall Trap

If a monster triggers a pitfall trap, it will become stuck and open to attack as it struggles to free itself.

Nerscylla Moveset

Arm Sweep

Using one of its arms, the Nerscylla will sweep in front of it to attack units in front of it.

Arm Thrust

Using either one of its arms, the Nerscylla will thrust downward to attack units in front of it.

Charged Attack

The Nerscylla backs away, pauses briefly, fires a web, and then throttles its body forward to charge at units in front of it.

Extended Arm Slice

The Nerscylla extends both of its arms and then proceeds to slice both inward, causing a scissor-like movement. This can blow away the hunter when hit directly.

Jump Attack

As the Nerscylla jumps high, it prepares to launch its body forward to land on the ground.

Nerscylla Hatchlings

Nerscylla calls on its offspring to join the fight. The tiny creatures can swarm Hunters if left unchecked.

Poison Stinger/h3>

The Nerscylla stings a target using its backside. When it hits the hunter, they will get the Poisoned status effect.

Swing and Dive

In an area where the Nerscylla can climb its webs, it will reach for the higher area, swing its body, and use the momentum to dive on a target.

Web Attack

The Nerscylla fires off lumps of web that wrap the hunter's body. When it hits a hunter, this move applies the "Webbed" effect and prevents the Hunter from moving.

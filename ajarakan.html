Species Description

Ajarakan is a Large Monster in Monster Hunter Wilds (MHWilds). Ajarakan is a fanged beast that may induce Fireblight, and it inhabits Oilwell Basin and Ruins of Wyveria. Large Monsters such as Ajarakan are hostile and are usually the primary objective of Hunts. They provide valuable Materials when defeated, that allow Hunters to craft gear.

Information

Characteristic by their highly developed carapaces, Ajarakan wrap themselves in molten metal that they rub together to heat up and create explosions. Presumably due to the friction of the metal, they can reach high enough temperatures to melt even the ground or bullets. They can also use their strong limbs to grip onto the ceiling and move around freely, so a hunter must watch out from every angle, including above.

Aliments Description Effectiveness

Poison

Poison slowly drains the monster's health for a short amount of time

Sleep

The monster slumbers a short while. Attacking will awaken it, but the first attack will do more damage.

Paralysis

The monster is paralyzed and cannot move for a short amount of time

Blastblight

Blast element builds as you attack. Once a certain threshold is reached, it detonates, causing damage.

Stun

Attacking a monster's head many times with a blunt weapon will KO them, leaving them wide open.

Exhaust

Once blunt damage buildup reaches a certain level, the monster will lose a portion of its stamina.

Items Description Effectiveness

Flash Pod

Strong Flashes will dizzy or disorient a monster for a short period of time.

Screamer pod

Some monsters are startled by loud noises, prompting specific reactions from them.

Shock Trap

If a monster triggers a shock trap, it will be immobilized and open to attack for a short amount of time.

Pitfall Trap

If a monster triggers a pitfall trap, it will become stuck and open to attack as it struggles to free itself.

Ajarakan Moveset

Shoulder Slam

Ajarakan will rise up with one shoulder in front and slam it down, followed by a charge while its shoulder is still on the ground and a charge upwards.

Projectile Toss

Ajarakan will dig its claws into the ground one at a time and swing it upward, releasing a projectile thrown towards you.

Ground Slam

Ajarakan raises both its claws before slamming both of them down onto the ground.

Lava Charge Up

Ajarakan digs both its claws into the ground and slams its back with its tail to heat itself up. Lastly, it bangs its fists against its chest.

Lava Ground Slam

It is the same as its Ground Slam attack, Ajarakan will release lava in front of it after it slams onto the ground.

Lava Shoulder Slam

It is the same as its Shoulder Slam, but will release lava upon impact on the ground and when it raises its shoulder up.

Lava Side Swipe

Ajarakan will dig its claws on the side and swing it horizontally across it and release a wall of lava.

Claw Charge

Ajarakan digs both its claws on the ground and charge forward.

Spin Charge

Ajarakan will spin in place before jumping up and spinning in the air, and darting down towards you causing an explosion of lava.

Grab Attack

When Ajarakan gets a hold of you, it will roar at you then begin to slam you onto the ground and toss you to the side

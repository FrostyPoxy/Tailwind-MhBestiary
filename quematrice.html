    Species Description

    Brute wyverns with disproportionately long tails. They spread a flammable substance which vaporizes quickly, then ignite it by dragging their tails along the ground. By nature, Quematrice rarely hunt their own prey, and instead scavenge herbivore carrion left behind by other predators. This often leads to confrontations with other, smaller carnivores.

    Information

    Quematrice is a Large Monster in Monster Hunter Wilds (MHWilds). Quematrice is a Brute Wyvern that resides in the Windward Plains and Ruins of Wyveria. Large Monsters such as Quematrice are hostile and are usually the primary objective of Hunts. They provide valuable Materials when defeated, that allow Hunters to craft gear.

    Aliments Description Effectiveness

    Poison

    Poison slowly drains the monster's health for a short amount of time

    Sleep

    The monster slumbers a short while. Attacking will awaken it, but the first attack will do more damage.

    Paralysis

    The monster is paralyzed and cannot move for a short amount of time

    Blastblight

    Blast element builds as you attack. Once a certain threshold is reached, it detonates, causing damage.

    Stun

    Attacking a monster's head many times with a blunt weapon will KO them, leaving them wide open.

    Exhaust

    Once blunt damage buildup reaches a certain level, the monster will lose a portion of its stamina.

    Items Description Effectiveness

    Flash Pod

    Strong Flashes will dizzy or disorient a monster for a short period of time.

    Screamer pod

    Some monsters are startled by loud noises, prompting specific reactions from them.

    Shock Trap

    If a monster triggers a shock trap, it will be immobilized and open to attack for a short amount of time.

    Pitfall Trap

    If a monster triggers a pitfall trap, it will become stuck and open to attack as it struggles to free itself.

    Quematrice Moveset

    Pecking Attack

    The Quematrice will perform a series of pecks with its beck. It can do this move up to 4 times in succession.

    Beak Sweep

    After pausing briefly, the Quematrice will perform a sweep attack from the side with its beak.

    Tail Slam

    The Quematrice will slam its feathery tail on the ground which can blow away units that it hits.

    Tail Sweep

    Using the tail, the Quematrice will perform a sweeping attack as it turns the whole body. It can blow away Palicoes if it hits them.

    Fireblight Tail Sweep

    This attack is similar to the Tail Sweep but is empowered by the Fire Element which ignites the ground hit by the ends of the tail.

    Rush Attack

    The Quematrice backs away, pauses briefly, and then rushes forward with its mouth wide open.

    Spin and Sweep

    This attack is a combination of a spinning move and a tail sweep at the end. The Quematrice will tuck its tail covering its hindleg and after spinning, it will sweep its tail upward.

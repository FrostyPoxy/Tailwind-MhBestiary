Species Description

Amphibians that use their long tongues to seize and devour prey such as Bulaqchi. Their evolved forelimbs store a sticky mucus-like substance. When agitated, Chatacabra rub their forelimbs against the ground to collect stone and ore, widening the range and destructiveness of their blows.

Information

Chatacabra is a Large Monster in Monster Hunter Wilds (MHWilds). Chatacabra is an amphibian that uses its long tongue to attack prey, and resides in the Windward Plains. Large Monsters such as Chatacabra are hostile and are usually the primary objective of Hunts. They provide valuable Materials when defeated, that allow Hunters to craft gear.

Aliments Description Effectiveness

Poison

Poison slowly drains the monster's health for a short amount of time

Sleep

The monster slumbers a short while. Attacking will awaken it, but the first attack will do more damage.

Paralysis

The monster is paralyzed and cannot move for a short amount of time

Blastblight

Blast element builds as you attack. Once a certain threshold is reached, it detonates, causing damage.

Stun

Attacking a monster's head many times with a blunt weapon will KO them, leaving them wide open.

Exhaust

Once blunt damage buildup reaches a certain level, the monster will lose a portion of its stamina.

Items Description Effectiveness

Flash Pod

Strong Flashes will dizzy or disorient a monster for a short period of time.

Screamer pod

Some monsters are startled by loud noises, prompting specific reactions from them.

Shock Trap

If a monster triggers a shock trap, it will be immobilized and open to attack for a short amount of time.

Pitfall Trap

If a monster triggers a pitfall trap, it will become stuck and open to attack as it struggles to free itself.

Chatacabra Moveset

Overhead Smash

The Chatacabra slowly backs away as it raises its right/left foreleg above its head to prepare for an Overhead attack. This attack is aimed directly at the unit in front of the Chatacabra.

Rush Attack

The Chatacabra backs away, pauses briefly, and then rushes forward with its mouth wide open and tongue hanging out.

Spin Attack

The Chatacabra spins in place while it drags its tongue on the ground beneath it.

Ground Stomp

After pausing for a bit, the Chatacabra will stomp the ground in a straight direction leaving dust behind its trails.

Dive

The Chatacabra wiggles its entire body to prepare for a jump and then lands on the ground with a frontal dive.

Roar

Lets out a roar that can deafen Hunters.
